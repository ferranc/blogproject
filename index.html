Politicians vs Citizens.
Are we talking about the same?

Since not so long ago, Spaniards seem more conscious about the fact that what our representatives are talking about is not what is worrying us. In this project we are going to compare what the politicians are debating in the Congress and what Spaniards are worried about.
We know that there are a lot of open questions, unfortunately, we had only two months and no time to answer all of them. These are the ones we have focus on:

  • Is what worries Spanish citizens the most what they talk about in the Congress?Or is what is more debated in Congress what later worries the citizens?
  • Do we have the same worries? How do they evolve?
  • What are they debating about?
  • Which political wing is more worried about current concerns?
  • Is there any external element that can affect it?

CIS: Centro de Investigaciones Sociológicas (Spanish Sociological Investigation Center)


The CIS is a public institution that carries out monthly sociological studies called barometers about the Spanish population.

It is very difficult to know what the main problems are in Spanish people opinion. However, the barometers offer us the perception of the Spanish population about the economical, sociological, political and other situations in the country. It is from here that we are going to take the information about Spanish people opinion.

Congreso de los Diputados
(Spanish Congress of Deputies)


The Congress is the lower house of the General Court, the Spanish legislative branch. It has 350 members that are elected every 4 years.

Despite the different types of sessions that we can find in the congress, we have chosen the Control Sessions. In those sessions is where different political groups ask the government about the concerns regarding the new laws or current issues of the country or the world.
