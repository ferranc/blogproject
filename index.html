CIS: Centro de Investigaciones Sociológicas (Spanish Sociological Investigation Center)


The CIS is a public institution that realises monthly sociological studies called barometers about the Spanish Population.

Is very difficult to know what are the main problems in opinion of the Spaniards. However, the barometers offer to us the perception of Spanish population about the economical, sociological, political and others situations in the country. It is from here from where we are going to take the information about the opinion of Spanish people.

Congreso de los Diputados
(Spanish Congress of Deputies)


The Congress is the lower house of the General Court, Spanish legislative branch. It has 350 members that are elected every 4 years.

Despite the different types of session that we can find in the congress, we have choosen the Control Sessions. Are in those sessions where de different political groups ask to the government about the concerns on the new laws or current issues of the country or the world
